Smart Appointment System

Enabling easy, flexible appointment bookings for patients and hospital teams.

In-Person Consultation (Scheduled)

  • Patients fill in basic details: Name, Email, Phone Number
  • Select doctor/specialty, date, and time
  • Payment can be made online or at the hospital

Online Video Consultation

  • Patients register online with basic details
  • Select doctor/specialty and preferred time slot
  • Make payment via Razorpay or hospital’s payment gateway
  • A secure video link is automatically generated and shared

Walk-In Consultation

  • No prior booking required
  • Front office registers the patient directly at the hospital reception

Walk-Ins
